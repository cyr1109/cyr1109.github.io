Teaching Experience


I encourage students to be active and engage with three dimensions of their learning experience: (1) group learning with fellow classmates, (2) mentored learning with instructors, and (3) self-learning through independent study. I provide them with a diverse set of channels where they can express their strengths, weaknesses, and ongoing progress. I not only encourage students to take advantage of their individually unique skills but also motivate them to improve upon their more uncertain abilities.
